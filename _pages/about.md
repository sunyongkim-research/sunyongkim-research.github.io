## About Me

I received my PhD in Finance from the Kellogg School of Management, Northwestern University in June 2024. I am currently an Assistant Professor in Finance and Business Economics at Fordham University Gabelli School of Business (GSB) since September 2026. Previously I held faculty positions at the University of Arizona and the Georgia Institute of Technology. 

My work is at the intersection of International Macro-Finance, Political Economy and Asset Pricing. Many of my solo papers have won best paper awards at top academic finance conferences such as the Western Finance Association (WFA), European Finance Association (EFA) and the American Finance Association (AFA). They are also currently going through the R&R process at top journals in econ and finance such as ReStud, JPE and AEJ Macro. Please review the tabs for further details on my work.
