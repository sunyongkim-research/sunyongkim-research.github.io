## About Me

I received my PhD in Finance from the Kellogg School of Management, Northwestern University in June 2024. I am currently a finance professor at the Eller College of Management, University of Arizona. I will be joining Fordham University as an Assistant Professor in Finance and Business Economics in September 2026.

My work is at the intersection of International Macro-Finance, Political Economy and Asset Pricing. Many of my solo papers are currently going through the R&R process at top journals in econ and finance such as ReStud, JPE and AEJ Macro.
